Kickdrum

Kickdrum is a full stack web application for sharing and discovering new music. Users browse songs in an Instagram-like stream of song images that are playable on click. The site supports persistent audio playback while the user navigates page to page. To share a song, users provide an image (i.e. an album cover) and a song via drag and drop upload or urls.
